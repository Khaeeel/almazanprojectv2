## Portfolio Structure

### Main Route (/)
The homepage features an immersive 3D experience with:
- **3D Character Model** - Interactive encrypted GLB model with mouse-following animations (Three.js + React Three Fiber)
- **Landing Section** - Name introduction with animated text
- **About Section** - Personal introduction
- **Career Section** - Timeline of academic/professional experience
- **Work Section** - Horizontal scroll showcase of top 5 projects
- **Tech Stack** - Inverted pyramid display of skills with video background
- **Certificates** - Grid display of achievements
- **Contact Section** - Contact information and social links

### Secondary Routes
